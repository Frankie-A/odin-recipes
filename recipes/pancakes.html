Pancakes

Pancakes with syrup

To make Pancakes you will first need to get bater, butter, milk, and eggs

 

You will pour the batter and the milk into a pan and mix them. After you will than mix in the eggs. Next throw that mix into a hot pan and flip both sides of the pancakes until ready. Lastly put butter on top of the pankes for extra flavor.

 

Ingridients

  • Bater
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  1. Mix bater with milk
  2. Mix eggs into new bater
  3. Pour new bater into hot pan
  4. Flip until both sides are ready
